About us

Brown Charcoal Pty Ltd is a charcoal and briquette producing factory which aims to create charcoal in a enviroment friendly fassion. We pride ourselves in the fact that we only use alien species trees for the burning of charcoal and use no chemicals in the process what so ever.

We are based in the Eastern Cape province of South Africa and being close to the harbour at Port Elizibeth, we are in the perfect position to export our products on large scale to countries that would be interested. We have a various suppliers of charcoal around the Eastern Cape. We receive the raw material from the suppliers and put it through our screening plant to sepperate the different sizes. Pieces that are the right size are weighd and packed. The smaller pieces are then put through a milling machine and gets grinded into a dust form. This dust is then mixed with water and other materials, which is then pressed into briquettes. The briquettes are then dryed several times untill they are ready to be packed and shipped. We offer custom packaging and sizes of packaging. We also cater for different sizes of product if nessacary.
